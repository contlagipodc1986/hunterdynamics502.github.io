#1 Free Video Editing Software Like iMovie - Shotcut

Shotcut is an open source and free video editing software developed by Meltytech LLC, which supports Mac, Windows and Linux. It follows the GNU/GPL protocol, which means anyone can use Shotcut for video editing and movie making for completely free.

Talking about video editing, Shotcut has functions such as GPU acceleration, broad format support, drag and drop operation, filter effects, etc. Like iMovie, Shotcut processes media files fast and does not occupy few computer resources. In my own experience using the software, Shotcut is an intermediate free video editor similar to iMovie. It's much easier than those professional video editing software such as Adobe Premiere, but it is slightly more complicated than other free video editing software with simple features. It can be said that Shotcut is the best of free video editor software. So it won our Gold price of iMovie-like free video editing software.

#2 Free Video Editing Software Like iMovie - OpenShot

The second iMovie alternative free video editing software is called OpenShot, a name similar to Shotcut.
