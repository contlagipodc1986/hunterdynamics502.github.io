How to Make the Cloned System Disk Boot Successfully?

If you just cloned system disk, you have two methods to ensure secure boot.

1. Shut down your machine, disconnect current hard drive using screwdrivers and keep only the cloned SSD connected and then boot from the cloned SSD.
