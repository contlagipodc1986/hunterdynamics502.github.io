Minotaur Task for Level 40+
With the release of the Summer Update 2014, new Minotaurs were added to the game. What led to an old concern mine. The Minotaur Task!
Current situation/Problem:
As you probably should know, you can't ask the task to the NPC Budrik after level 40, what makes unfair for who didn't know. My case for example. I just knew about the task at level 55. NPC Budrik claims that we are to stronger to kill minotaurs and we wouldn't like to do it. It can be true... for the weakest minotaurs.
Solutions:
So, my proposal is at level 100 (for example) be able to ask NPC Budrik a new task to kill only the new minotaurs (5000?) that are stronger and suitable even for my level. That would bring for me a new chance to get the nose ring. And, of course, this task can't be done for all. It can't be done if:
You had already done the previous task (40-)
You have the previous task open
Goals:
A new chance to make the task and get the nose ring
A new goal to achieve in Tibia
Dynamic task system
A new way to incentive hunt in the new area
Outcome:
It will be a challenger and expensive to knights finish the task.
Horned Fox isn't a big challenge for level 100+
Consequences:
Decrease the 'rarity' of the nose ring
Decrease of the price of the nose ring
Assassin Outfit will be easier to get
Other Proposals
Create a new boss with suitable items for high levels (by Dekraken durinars)
This is just a proposal, and i just put here to see what you think about it. It can be discuss a way to improve the proposal.
